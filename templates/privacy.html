1. Overview

CampusConnect ("the Platform," "we," "us") is an AI-powered campus social platform exclusively for students, faculty, and staff of Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College (SREC), Coimbatore. The platform is accessible at connectsrec.in.

By creating an account and using CampusConnect, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. This policy applies to all users — students, faculty, and administrators.

Platform Scope: CampusConnect is strictly limited to users with a valid @srec.ac.in email address. We do not allow public access or registration from outside SREC.

2. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data when you use CampusConnect:

Category What We Collect When
Account Info SREC email address, role (student/faculty/admin), display name, bio At signup & profile update
Posts & Content Text posts, images, tags, anonymous post preference, likes, bookmarks, reactions When you post or interact
Comments & Replies Comment text, reply text, timestamps, usernames When you comment or reply
Notes & PDFs Uploaded PDF files, subject, description, uploader name, download count, ratings When you upload to Notes Hub
Study Room What you're currently studying, duration, username When you join Study Room (auto-expires in 4 hours)
Quiz Data Quiz scores, subject, room ID, username When you participate in Room Quiz
Chat Messages Messages sent to the SREC AI chatbot When you use the chatbot
Usage Data Login timestamps, session activity Automatically during use

Anonymous Posts: When you choose to post anonymously, your username is hidden from other users. However, your identity is still linked to the post internally in our database for moderation purposes.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Account authentication — verifying your identity and SREC email during login and signup
  • Platform functionality — displaying posts, comments, notes, events, study rooms, and quiz results
  • AI features — your chatbot messages are sent to Groq's API (LLaMA 3.3-70B) to generate responses; your post content may be used for Post Enhancer and Campus Pulse AI summaries
  • Leaderboard & stats — tracking post likes to display the top contributors leaderboard
  • Platform improvement — understanding how features are used to improve the platform
  • Moderation — enabling admins to manage inappropriate content

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third party for advertising or commercial purposes.

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored across the following infrastructure:

  • Firebase Realtime Database — stores posts, comments, events, study room data, quiz results, user profiles, and bookmarks. Data is hosted on Google's secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Firebase Authentication — manages your email, password (hashed), and session tokens. Passwords are never stored in plain text.
  • Supabase Storage — stores PDF notes uploaded to the Notes Hub. Files are stored on Supabase's secure PostgreSQL-backed storage.
  • Cloudinary — stores images attached to posts. Images are processed and optimized automatically.
  • Render — the Flask application server hosting CampusConnect is deployed on Render's cloud platform.

Security Measures: All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Passwords are managed by Firebase Authentication and are never stored by CampusConnect directly. Session cookies are HTTP-only and expire after 24 hours.

6. Who Can Access Your Data

  • You — can see all your own posts, comments, bookmarks, and profile data
  • Other logged-in SREC users — can see your non-anonymous posts, username, and notes you've shared
  • Faculty & Admin users — can see all posts (including anonymous poster identity for moderation) and manage events
  • CampusConnect administrators — have full database access for platform maintenance and moderation
  • Third-party services — only receive the minimum data required to provide their service (e.g., Groq only receives your chat message, not your email)

Your data is never accessible to users outside SREC — the platform requires a valid @srec.ac.in login for any access.

7. AI & Automated Processing

CampusConnect uses AI for the following features:

  • SREC AI Chatbot — your messages are sent to Groq's LLaMA 3.3-70B model to generate responses about SREC and general queries
  • Post Enhancer — your draft post text is sent to Groq to suggest an improved version; you choose whether to use it
  • Campus Pulse — today's posts are collectively analyzed by AI to generate a mood summary; individual posts are not attributed in the output
  • Room Quiz — your chosen subject is sent to Groq to generate quiz questions
  • Sentiment Analysis — posts may be analyzed for positive/negative/neutral sentiment to display badges

AI Disclaimer: AI-generated responses (chatbot, quiz questions, post suggestions) may not always be accurate. Do not rely on AI responses for critical academic or administrative decisions. Always verify with official SREC sources.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Posts & Comments — retained until you delete them or an admin removes them for policy violations
  • Study Room data — automatically deleted after 4 hours of inactivity
  • Notes/PDFs — retained until you delete them from the Notes Hub
  • Account data — retained as long as your account is active
  • Session data — expires after 24 hours of inactivity

To request complete deletion of your account and all associated data, contact us at the email below. We will process deletion requests within 7 working days.

9. Your Rights

As a CampusConnect user, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data stored on the platform
  • Correction — update your profile, bio, and posted content at any time
  • Deletion — delete your posts, comments, and notes; request full account deletion
  • Withdrawal — stop using the platform at any time; your data will remain until deletion is requested
  • Objection — raise concerns about how your data is processed by contacting us
