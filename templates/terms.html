1. Eligibility & Access

CampusConnect is exclusively available to:

  • Currently enrolled students of Sri Ramakrishna Engineering College (SREC)
  • Faculty and staff of SREC with a valid institutional email
  • Administrators authorized by the college

Registration requires a valid @srec.ac.in email address. Attempts to register with non-institutional emails will be rejected. You must not share your login credentials with anyone outside SREC.

By registering, you confirm that you are a current member of the SREC community and that the information you provide is accurate.

2. Account Responsibilities

  • You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your password and all activity under your account
  • You must not share your account with others or allow unauthorized access
  • You must not create multiple accounts for the same person
  • You must not impersonate other students, faculty, or college officials
  • If you suspect unauthorized access to your account, change your password immediately and notify us

CampusConnect is not responsible for any loss or damage arising from your failure to maintain account security.

3. Acceptable Use & Community Rules

CampusConnect is a respectful academic community. The following rules apply to all users:

You Should
  • Post constructive, helpful, and respectful content
  • Share relevant study materials and notes
  • Support and encourage fellow students
  • Report content that violates these terms
  • Use the platform for academic and campus life purposes
You Must Not
  • Harass, bully, or intimidate any user
  • Post hate speech, discriminatory, or offensive content
  • Share spam, irrelevant, or commercial content
  • Post false information about SREC or its members
  • Attempt to hack, exploit, or disrupt the platform
  • Share another person's private information without consent

Violations of community rules may result in content removal, account suspension, or permanent ban. Serious violations may be reported to the college administration.

  • You own the content or have the right to share it

  • The content is not defamatory, obscene, threatening, or illegal

By posting content, you grant CampusConnect a non-exclusive license to display and store that content on the platform for the purpose of operating the service.

Anonymous Posts: While your name is hidden from other users when posting anonymously, your identity remains linked to your post in our database for moderation purposes. Anonymous posting does not exempt you from these Terms of Use.

5. Notes Hub Policy

The Notes Hub allows students to share study materials. When uploading notes, you agree that:

  • You are the original creator of the notes, or have the right to share them
  • The notes do not contain confidential exam papers or answer keys obtained through unauthorized means
  • The notes are relevant to SREC coursework and academic subjects
  • You are not violating any faculty or institutional copyright

Uploading unauthorized exam papers, leaked question papers, or plagiarized content is strictly prohibited and may be reported to the college. Admins have the right to remove any notes that violate this policy.

  • AI responses are generated automatically and may not always be accurate or complete

  • You should not rely solely on AI responses for academic, administrative, or medical decisions
  • Your chat messages and post content sent to AI features are processed by Groq's API per their privacy policy
